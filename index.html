About Medi-Tech Laboratory

Medi-Tech Laboratory is a licensed clinical diagnostic centre in Kerala, offering comprehensive pathology and laboratory testing services. We are committed to delivering accurate diagnostics that support effective treatment, preventive care, and improved health outcomes.

Services
